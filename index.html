01 — The Problem

Your people data is everywhere. None of it is connected.

Candidate histories in your ATS. Relationship context in recruiters' inboxes. Alumni networks no one can query. Lateral talent moving across the market with no visibility into who's qualified, available, and who fits.

Every hiring cycle starts from scratch. Every AI initiative stalls because the underlying data isn't structured or contextualized enough to be useful.

The problem isn't that your firm lacks people data. It's that your people data doesn't work.

People data that compounds in value over time — the way your best investment research does.

Arbour is building the people intelligence infrastructure for financial services. A structured, contextualized graph of human capital — internal and external — that gets more powerful the more your firm uses it. Not a tool. A foundation.

02 — How it works

One platform. Three layers.

Built specifically for financial services — where the meaning behind people data is as important as the data itself.

01

People graph

A proprietary network of structured people data covering students, professionals, and lateral candidates across financial services. Internal and external, unified.

02

Context layer

AI can read data. Arbour helps it understand what that data means — what a role signals, what seniority looks like at a fund versus a bank. Domain knowledge built in.

03

Intelligence layer

AI agents and custom applications that turn your structured people graph into decisions — faster sourcing, smarter screening, and use cases you haven't had the infrastructure to pursue.

Common questions

Things worth knowing before you reach out.

Student and consumer data on the Arbour platform is fully opt-in — individuals control what they share. Third-party data is sourced from licensed providers under established data agreements. We are designed for the compliance requirements of financial services from day one.
Client internal data never touches Arbour's shared infrastructure. Your people data is processed in isolated environments with enterprise-grade security controls. We work within your existing security review and procurement processes.
Data services engagements are structured as an implementation fee followed by a recurring subscription covering data licensing and the applications we build together. Software tiers are separately priced. We scope every engagement based on your data environment and use cases — contact us to get a sense of fit.
