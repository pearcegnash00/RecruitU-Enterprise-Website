How it works

One platform. Three layers. Built for financial services.

People graph

A proprietary network of structured people data covering students, professionals, and lateral candidates across financial services. Internal and external, unified in one place.

Context layer

AI can read data. Arbour helps it understand what that data means. What a role signals. What seniority looks like at a fund versus a bank. The domain knowledge that makes people intelligence accurate.

Intelligence layer

AI agents and custom applications that turn your structured people graph into decisions — faster sourcing, smarter screening, and use cases your team hasn't had the infrastructure to pursue.

Data services engagements are structured as an implementation fee followed by a recurring subscription covering data licensing and the applications we build together.
